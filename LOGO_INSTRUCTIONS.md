# How to Add Your Logo & Video

## 📷 Logo Setup

### Step 1: Prepare Your Logo
1. Save your logo image as `logo.png`
2. For best results, use a PNG file with transparent background
3. Recommended dimensions: 300-400px wide, maintaining aspect ratio

### Step 2: Add Logo to Project
1. Navigate to the `public` folder in your project
2. Replace the empty `logo.png` file with your actual logo image
3. Keep the filename as `logo.png`

**Logo Location:**
```
/public/logo.png
```

**Where the Logo Appears:**
- **Navigation Bar** (top of every page)
- **Footer** (bottom of every page)

The logo will automatically scale and display correctly on all devices!

## 🎥 Hero Video Setup

### Step 1: Prepare Your Video
1. Save your video as `hero-video.mp4`
2. **Recommended specs:**
   - Format: MP4 (H.264 codec)
   - Resolution: 1920x1080 (Full HD) or higher
   - Duration: 10-30 seconds (it will loop automatically)
   - File size: Keep under 10MB for fast loading
   - Aspect ratio: 16:9 (landscape)

### Step 2: Add Video to Project
1. Navigate to the `public` folder in your project
2. Replace the empty `hero-video.mp4` file with your actual video
3. Keep the filename as `hero-video.mp4`

**Video Location:**
```
/public/hero-video.mp4
```

**Video Features:**
- ✅ Auto-plays on page load (muted)
- ✅ Loops continuously
- ✅ Beautiful dark overlay for text readability
- ✅ Responsive on all devices
- ✅ Elegant gradient overlay

**Tips for Best Results:**
- Use a video showing compassionate care moments
- Ensure good lighting in the video
- Avoid fast movements (smooth, calm footage works best)
- The video will have a dark overlay, so bright scenes work well
